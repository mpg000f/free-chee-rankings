1
🔥Scampi: Gallo (with hair)
Gallo
—

In a matchup of the top two teams in the league, Scampi left zero doubt. The craziest part is it was mostly a mediocre day by much of Scampi’s roster. Darnold was bad with 6 points. James Cook had only 8. None of that mattered because Jonathan “Tishman” Taylor went for a fucking million. Taylor had the 6th best half-ppr performance by a running back in the last 10 years. One of those top five was Taylor himself in 2021 by the way. The man is so clearly the best player in fantasy football. After the game, one of our reporters had the privilege of asking Taylor about his performance and he responded, “I almost felt bad. Getting into their 2nd level was almost as easy as putting back a 30 piece from Chick Fil A.” Wise words from the best player in the league. Taylor is down to +500 for NFL MVP. At 9-1 and a point differential similar to the 07 Pats, Scampi is an overwhelming favorite to be the 1st seed in the playoffs. Our model pegs it as a 83% chance for Scampi. With Marcus Mariota clearly going to be a serviceable QB2 going forward, this team is unbelievable. The only worry for Scampi is if he’s peaking too soon. Can Taylor continue this tour de force for another two months?

Art of the Deal
Matt
—

Art ran into an absolute buzz saw this week against Scampi, but we remain confident this team will be a force to be reckoned with come playoff time. With a matchup against the reeling Team Daniel up next, it’s likely Art of the Deal gets back in the win column and moves to 8-3 next week.

Cookie Monster Golf Cart

The committee continues to look like a crew of Nostradamus, George Orwell and the Oracle of Delphi for its faith in CMGC. In his 7 starts, Dart is averaging 23.3 points per game. That is tied with Josh Allen for 2nd best in the league on a per game basis. Dart is a stud. If only the Giants would fire Daboll. Perhaps they can just demote him to running the hot dog cart in the 2nd concourse for next year. At 4-6, CMGC sits with only a 44% to make the playoffs but if he gets in, he might be the only team Scampi truly fears. Our model has him at a 12% chance to win the title despite his low playoff odds.

Pigs on the 7th Rank
Deez
▲ 1

Pigs got waxed pretty good this week through no fault of his own. CMGC just took a chainsaw to his defense, and it was fruitless to protest. Having said that, the committee likes how he has put this team together. Pigs correctly identified that Mahomes and H-Dubz were undervalued in the preseason and they have delivered big time for him. Since Dowdle’s breakout in week five, he is the RB4 overall. He is behind only Jonathan Taylor, Christian McCaffrey and De’Von Achane. Is he kidding? In addition to Dowdle, Jaylen Waddle has taken his game up a level since Tyreek’s injury. Since week five, Waddle is WR6 in total points. Those two will need to keep it up for Pigs to reach their goals. Given the injuries he has faced, most on the committee will consider a simple playoff berth a successful season, but rumors suggest Bloomfield is aiming higher.
